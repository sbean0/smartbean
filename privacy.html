[ PRIVACY POLICY ]

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Information Collection

We believe in data minimization. The only personal information we collect is what you voluntarily submit through our "Collaboration Channel" forms (such as an optional Identity String and Reply Address).

2. How We Use Your Data

Information submitted through our forms is used exclusively to evaluate collaboration ideas and, if a Reply Address is provided, to respond to your transmission. We utilize third-party routing (such as Formsubmit) to process these messages securely.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to outside parties. Your data remains internal to Smartbean Ai for operational and collaborative purposes only.

4. Cookies & Tracking

This primary node (smartbean.ai) operates as a static terminal and does not use tracking cookies. Linked sub-projects (like Smartly.Academy) may have their own specific localized privacy and tracking policies.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please transmit a message via the main Collaboration Channel or contact forms@smartbean.ai.
